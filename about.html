Stay guidelines

Rules for smooth check-in and stay.

  • Standard check-in starts after 14:00 and check-out is by 12:00.
  • Room allocation is confirmed after successful booking/payment completion.
  • Please keep a valid government ID ready at check-in.
  • Respect shared spaces and quiet hours to keep the hostel comfortable for all guests.
