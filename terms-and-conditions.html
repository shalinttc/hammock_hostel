Terms and Conditions

These terms outline the basic rules for booking and staying at Hammock Hostels. Property-specific directions shared during booking or check-in also form part of your stay guidelines.

1. Bookings and confirmations

All reservations are subject to availability and become valid after confirmation on the official booking platform. The final room type, occupancy, applicable taxes, and inclusions are shown during checkout and in the confirmation sent to the guest.

2. Check-in requirements

Guests may be asked to present a valid government-issued photo ID at check-in. Check-in and check-out timings, bed allocations, and access instructions may vary by property and operational schedule.

3. Stay conduct

Guests are expected to respect fellow travelers, property staff, and house rules. Any behavior that affects safety, causes disturbance, damages property, or violates local laws may result in refusal of service or early checkout, subject to management discretion.

4. Payments, changes, and extras

Booking modifications, extensions, and special requests are subject to availability. Additional services, incidental charges, or on-property purchases that are not part of the original booking may be charged separately.

5. Personal belongings and liability

Guests are responsible for their personal belongings during the stay. Hammock Hostels recommends using lockers or secure storage where available and is not responsible for loss, theft, or damage except where required by applicable law.

6. Policy updates

Hammock Hostels may revise these terms from time to time to reflect operational, legal, or service updates. Continued use of the website, booking engine, or property stay indicates acceptance of the latest version in force at that time.
