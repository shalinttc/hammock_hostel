Policies

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation and refund terms can vary by property, travel dates, and the rate selected at the time of booking. The policy displayed during checkout always remains the final reference for that reservation.

1. Rate-specific cancellation rules

Each booking may carry its own cancellation window and charge conditions. Promotional, peak-date, event, long stay, or group reservations may have stricter rules than standard flexible bookings.

2. Modifications before arrival

Changes to check-in dates, stay duration, room type, or occupancy are subject to availability and prevailing rates. A modification request may result in a fare difference or a fresh cancellation policy being applied.

3. Late cancellations and no-shows

If a booking is cancelled after the applicable free cancellation window, or if the guest does not arrive without notice, charges may apply according to the selected rate plan. This may include the first night, a partial charge, or the full booking amount where specified.

4. Refund timelines

Approved refunds are generally processed back to the original payment source. Actual credit timelines may vary depending on the bank, card provider, or payment gateway involved in the original transaction.

5. Third-party bookings

If a reservation was made through a third-party platform, cancellation requests and refunds may need to be handled through that same platform, in line with their process and the booking terms agreed there.

6. Need assistance?

For help with an existing reservation, contact the Hammock Hostels team with your booking details so the team can guide you using the exact rate conditions attached to your reservation.
